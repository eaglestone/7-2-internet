Eggs on Toast

Scrambled eggs can be one of the most fantastic, luxurious breakfasts around if you remember a few key things: always use really fresh free-range or organic eggs and good-quality butter, and always keep the eggs moving slowly over a low heat. Do this and you’ll get luscious, buttery, creamy eggs every time.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs, preferably free-range or organic
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper a good lump of butter, plus extra to serve
  • slices of bread

To cook your eggs

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper
  3. Beat well with a fork
  4. Put a small pan over a low heat and add a good lump of butter
  5. Melt it slowly until it’s frothy
  6. Pour the eggs into the pan and stir them slowly – use a wooden spoon or a spatula if you have one, so you can get right into the sides of the pan
  7. While you’re doing this, pop some bread on to toast
