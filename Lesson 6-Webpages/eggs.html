Eggs on Toast Scrambled eggs can be one of the most fantastic, luxurious breakfasts around if you remember a few key things: always use really fresh free-range or organic eggs and good-quality butter, and always keep the eggs moving slowly over a low heat. Do this and you’ll get luscious, buttery, creamy eggs every time. Ingredients 4 large eggs, preferably free-range or organic sea salt and freshly ground black pepper a good lump of butter, plus extra to serve 4 slices of bread To cook your eggs Crack your eggs into a bowl Add a pinch of salt and pepper Beat well with a fork Put a small pan over a low heat and add a good lump of butter Melt it slowly until it’s frothy Pour the eggs into the pan and stir them slowly – use a wooden spoon or a spatula if you have one, so you can get right into the sides of the pan While you’re doing this, pop some bread on to toast Taken from: http://www.jamieshomecookingskills.com/recipe.php?title=scrambled-eggs-on-toast
